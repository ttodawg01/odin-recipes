Chorizo

Description

Chorizo is a spanish sausage primarly of pork and is versatile! You can mix chorizo with eggs, cook chorizo on the grill and also mix with beans as well

Ingridients

  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Pepper and Paprika
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. You must cook the chorizo for about 10-15 minutes or until golden brown (Olive oil is optional when cooking small quantities of chorizo is cooked)
  2. Add paprika until your liking
  3. cook some corn tortillas and serve!

Asada

Description

Carne asada is a very tender cut of beef that is moutwatering when seasoned correctly!

ingridients

  • 2-3 pounds of asada
  • beer and adobo seasoning
  • a charcoal grill

Steps

  1. You must marinade the asada with the beer and adobo seasoning
  2. prep your grill with charcoal for about 10 minutes
  3. lay your asada on the grill and serve when ready!
